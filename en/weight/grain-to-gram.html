Weight

Weight is the amount or quantity of heaviness or mass (the amount a thing weighs).

Imperial / American measurements

Examples of Imperial / American measurements are ounce(oz), pound(lbs) and stone (st).

Metric measurements

Examples of Metric measurements are Kilograms (Kg), grams and metic tonnes.

Grain

The unified atomic mass unit (symbol: u) or dalton (symbol: Da) is the standard unit that is used for indicating mass on an atomic or molecular scale. In imprecise terms, one AMU is the average of the proton rest mass and the neutron rest mass. This is approximately 1.67377 x 10 -27 kilogram (kg), or 1.67377 x 10 -24 gram (g). The mass of an atom in AMU is roughly equal to the sum of the number of protons and neutrons in the nucleus.
