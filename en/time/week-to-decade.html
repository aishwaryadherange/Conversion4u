Time

Time is the measure of durations of events and the intervals between them.

The SI unit of time is the second. There are 60 seconds in a minute, 3600 seconds in an hour and 86400 seconds in an hour.

Week

A week is a time unit equal to seven days. It is the standard time period used for cycles of work days and rest days in most parts of the world. There are 604800 seconds or 168 hours in a week.

Decade

A decade is a period of 10 years. The word is derived (via French and Latin) from the Ancient Greek dekas which means ten. A decade has 315569260 seconds and 87658.1278 hours.
